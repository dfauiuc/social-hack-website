ABOUT US


We are a national network of college students using design-thinking for social good. We are passionate.

Design for America at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign is a registered 501(c)3 non-profit that focuses on the human-centered design process. Our studio is part of a larger award-winning national network of passionate creators & innovators revolutionizing the way university students engage with & improve the world around us. We seek to build interdiscplinary teams, which means we are open to students of all majors. Anyone interested in design, innovation, entrepreneurship, or just making a difference can help us tackle a wide variety of challenges.
